Carlos Villalobos


Welcome to my home page. I am a researcher and Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Talca. My research focuses on the topics of poverty, income inequality, gender inequality, health economics, and energy economics.


I hold a PhD Economics from the Georg-August Universität Göttingen in Germany. I also obtained a MS equivalent degree in Economics from the Universidad de Talca in Chile. On the other hand, I am affiliated with the Ibero-American Institute for Economic Research at the University of Göttingen in Germany. I have also served as a senior consultant for the World Bank, European Commission, IDB, USAID (US), DFID (UK), UNDP, UNICEF and many governments.
